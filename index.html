About Us

At Atithe Porayon, we are dedicated to preserving the authentic flavors of Porabari, Tangail, through our traditional sweets. With a passion for sharing the rich cultural heritage of our region, we specialize in crafting delicious chomchom and creamy doi, made from the finest ingredients. Every sweet we offer is a tribute to time-honored recipes passed down through generations, delivering a taste of history with every bite.

Our commitment to quality ensures that you experience the true essence of Porabari’s sweet legacy, made fresh with love and care. We take pride in bringing you the best of tradition, creating a memorable experience with every treat.
